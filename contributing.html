Contributing

Do you have a resource to share? Pull Requests are very welcome. If you know of a good resource or tool for managers, you can just PR a link to it under its category on the Tools page.

If you are sharing something to be included directly on The Soft Stuff and it's not already available online, please note that content stored directly on this site is openly-licensed. You can specify a different license or no license at all - please indicate your choice in the PR, or I'll assume you want it released under our license.

If you are not a github user and want to share or post something to The Soft Stuff, please contact me on email: jwrubel (at) gmail dot com or twitter and I'll be happy to help review and incorporate your change in whatever method makes the most sense, even if that's just a link to something you've already published.
